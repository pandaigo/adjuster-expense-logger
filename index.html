What it does

One claim, one tag

Every per diem, hotel night, mileage trip, meal, parking fee, supply, and phone bill gets tagged to the IA company's claim number. Subtotal per claim instantly.

Mileage auto-calc

Type the miles, pick the Mileage category — the dollar amount is auto-filled at the 2026 IRS standard rate of $0.725/mi. Override the rate in Settings when the IRS updates it.

CSV & PDF export

Export the filtered view as CSV for QuickBooks / Excel / accountant handoff (Free). Pro adds a PDF report with per-category and per-claim subtotals — universal format that works for any IA firm filing.

Receipts, the way they come

Paste $1,234.50 or 1,234.50 straight from a Marriott receipt or QuickBooks CSV. Dollar signs, thousands separators, and US date formats (5/12/2026) are all accepted.

Deployment header

Adjuster name, CAT event ("Hurricane Helene 2025"), and start / end dates appear on every export. Auditors and IA companies get the context they need on page one.

Your data, locally

Everything is stored in chrome.storage.local on your device. No account login, no cloud sync, no analytics. The only outbound connection is to ExtensionPay when you choose to upgrade.
